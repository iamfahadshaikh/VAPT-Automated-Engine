                        SCORING EXAMPLE
================================================================================

Finding: /api/users?id injectable

Factors:
  • Payload Success Rate: 95% (actual execution proof)
  • Corroboration Count: 3 tools (sqlmap, nuclei, burp)
  • Tool Agreement: High (avg 0.92 reliability)
  • OWASP Category: A03_INJECTION (High impact)
  • Privilege Level: ADMIN (authenticated)

Calculation:
  raw_score = (0.95 × 0.30) + (1.0 × 0.30) + (0.92 × 0.20) + (0.95 × 0.20)
            = 0.285 + 0.30 + 0.184 + 0.19
            = 0.959

  final_score = 0.959 × 2.0 (HIGH impact) × 1.5 (ADMIN privilege)
              = 2.877 → clamped to 1.0

Result: ✅ CRITICAL
